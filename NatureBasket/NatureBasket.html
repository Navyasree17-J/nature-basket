Open Government Data

AI Price Agent

Our AI agent analyzes market patterns and gives personalised advice — when to sell, where to sell, and what price to expect.

Historical Analysis

Up to 5 years of price history with seasonal breakdown so you can predict and plan crop cycles better.

Price Alerts

Set target prices and get SMS/WhatsApp alerts when your crop hits the right rate at any mandi.

District Intelligence

Color-coded maps show which districts are paying more — so you can travel to the right mandi and maximise returns.
